How It Works

Client Proposes A deal

The client in this case is a person who requires a service, or a buyer. He proposes a deal by providing a contract document.

Contract Value is Locked

During deal creation the contract value is also provided which is locked in the escrow contract

Service provider Accepts Deal

The service provider is invited to the deal, and is expected to accept or reject the deal.

Service Provider Deliver

The timer starts counting as soon as the service provider accepts the deal. And is expected to provide the service with time frame

Client Confirms Service

After delivery of service the client is expected to confirm service delivery within the extension period, provided by client during deal creation.

Dispute

The client can dispute the service delivered for a number of times he specified during deal creation, in which the service provider is suppose to redo

Validators

Validators are invloved after a dispute cant be resolved by both parties. Validators are experts in the field of service, who go over the deal and decides through a vote.

Settlement

Depending on the outcome of the deal processes leading to validation funds are released to either the client or provider depending on the outcome.

Excalation

This is a case where gorvernance gets involved in case validation wasnt successful dependending on various factors.
